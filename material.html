  1. Dinâmica e sistemas dinâmicos (in Portuguese). Includes lecture notes, solved problems, exams and summaries of the lectures. An English translation: Dynamics and Dynamical Systems is in progress.
  2. Eletricidade, magnetismo e circuitos (in Portuguese). Includes lecture notes, solved problems, exams and summaries of the lectures. An English translation: Eletricity, Magnetism and Circuits is in progress.
  3. The Michelson Interferometer. Animated slides to explain how a Michelson interferometer works.
  4. Exposição de experiências sobre a luz (in Portuguese). The catalog for an exhibition on historical experiments about light, held at the Library of the Engineering School, University of Porto, from 4/2005 to 6/2005 (within the commemorations of the International Year of Physics, 2005).
