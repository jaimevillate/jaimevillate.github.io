Conclusions


  • The method shown has been used successfully during the past 14 years to assess first and second year engineering students.
  • The availability of the exams online has improved the motivation of the students and their results.
  • The results of the surveys conducted among students in the past years are proof of the success of the method.
  • For a class of 200 students, the results of an exam with 2 problems and 15 multiple-choice questions are normally published within 24 hours (2 or 3 teachers doing the grading).
  • Although the Maxima program and the Moodle module described are not in a stable form that can be distributed, they are Free Software and available in beta form for anyone interested.
