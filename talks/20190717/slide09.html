Written exams graded on-line


In my teaching I have used an approach that combines written exams with online quizzes.

  • A written exam is used, with both problems and multiple-choice questions.
  • Several different versions are created with basically the same questions, differing on the parameters or expressions used.
  • Problems and questions are generated with the help of a Computer Algebra System.
  • Exams are produced in two formats: LaTeX for printing, and XML to be imported into the e-learning system.
  • After the exam, teachers grade the exams in an e-learning system.
  • Students can review their graded exams in the e-learning platform.
