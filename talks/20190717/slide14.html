Exam grading in Moodle


I have also written a new Moodle module, exam, based on the quiz standard module.

  • Creates Moodle quizzes that students cannot answer. Only view them after they have been "answered" by the teachers.
  • When the XML version of the exam is imported into Moodle, the questions are added to the standard questions database.
  • The XML exam is also imported into the exam module, leaving it ready to be graded by the teachers.
  • The teacher selects the student to evaluate, from a list of participants, copies the multiple-choice answers, and writes down a grade and comments for the problems.
  • Once the answers are copied, the final grade is computed automatically and the student can see the result, as if it had been answered by him in Moodle.
